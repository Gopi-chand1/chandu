##  Steps for Execution:- 



Step_1 :- You have Visual Studio 2019.

Step_2 :- Click on the open existing folder or project.

Step_3 :- Open a file which has .sln extention

Step_4 :- Press F5 or excute button to run the Application.

Step_5 :- In CMD
o	Displays Notification System. There should be 2 columns, one for Email and one for SMS. 
o	Press 1 for Email then you can see Subject and Body of Meassage.
o	Press 2 for SMS then you can see Subject and Body of Meassage.
o	Fill Subject and Body of Meassage as your wish then click Enter.
o	You can see the Email or SMS send to Subscribed Users.
